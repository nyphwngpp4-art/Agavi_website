## Why Monster Air is different from every other prospect

Monster Air is **client zero**. The goal is not to maximize revenue on this one engagement — it's to produce a bankable case study that closes the next 20 Phoenix HVAC shops.

That means every decision on this engagement is optimized for **proof**, not margin:

- Heavily discounted (or free) pricing in exchange for case-study rights
- Deliberately chosen wedge workflow (phone recovery) because it produces the most dramatic before/after
- Before-the-fact measurement so the case study is defensible
- Explicit permission to use name, logo, numbers, and a short video testimonial

If Monster Air goes well, the next 12 months of Agavi's pipeline is built on a single PDF and a 60-second Loom. That's worth discounting a pilot for.

## The scope — what Monster Air actually gets

### Week 1: Baseline measurement
- Pull 14 days of historical call data (before-state)
- Generate the Missed Call Audit report for Monster Air's real numbers
- 30-min walkthrough of current call handling at the shop
- Document current CSR workflow (video or written)

### Week 2: Install
- Set up AI receptionist on existing phone system (RingCentral / OpenPhone / whichever they use)
- Integration with existing scheduler (ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro / whatever they're on)
- Custom voice, custom greeting, custom call flow
- Configure after-hours emergency text escalation to owner
- Test with 10 live calls before going live

### Weeks 3–4: Live + measurement
- Daily check-ins for the first 5 days
- Review call recordings, fix edge cases
- Generate after-state report comparing week 1 baseline to week 4 live numbers
- **This delta is the case study.**

### Week 5: Case study production
- 60-minute on-site session to record before/after numbers
- Record a 60-second video testimonial (if results justify it)
- Write a one-page case study: "Monster Air recovered $X in its first 21 days with Agavi"
- Get explicit written permission for: name, logo, numbers, photo, video, anonymized call examples

### Month 6+: Retainer kicks in
- Starting October 1 (after summer's over and the numbers are bulletproof)
- $495/mo for months 6–12
- $850/mo starting month 13
- Month-to-month, cancel anytime

## Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Monster Air's phone system is an old legacy PBX that can't integrate | Ask on the pre-meeting call: "What phone system are you on?" If it's legacy, scope week 1 to also migrate them to OpenPhone or RingCentral and charge a small additional fee for the migration ($500–$1,500). |
| Owner agrees but office manager pushes back | Get the office manager on the week 1 walkthrough. Bring donuts. Explain that the AI handles overflow, not replacement. Make her an ally, not a speed bump. |
| Results are mediocre (20–40% miss reduction instead of 80%) | Do not publish the case study. Go back to drawing board, figure out why, fix it. Better no case study than a weak one. |
| Monster Air loves it and refers 3 shops before the case study is done | Accept the referrals at full retail ($1,950 per install). Do not discount again. The first case study is the only one that gets the freebie. |
| Owner changes his mind about video testimonial mid-engagement | Written case study + anonymized numbers is still enough. Don't push. |
