AGV—P02
◇ Why this is different

The website isn't the work.
The walkthrough is the work.

Every local web shop will build you a site and vanish. During Foundation delivery, we sit with you and trace exactly where leads enter, where calls go unreturned, and which evenings you're spending answering the same three questions.

01 — Lead flow

Where do they come from? Where do they fall through?

Phone, web form, Google Business, Facebook DM, referral. We map each entry point and the response time on each one. Most owners are surprised which channel is actually broken.

02 — Scheduling

How many phone tags before a confirmed appointment?

We count the round-trips. If it's three, four, five — that's where AI scheduling pays for itself in the first month.

03 — Follow-up

What happens after a job is done?

Review request? Re-engagement at 90 days? Referral ask? Most local businesses leave the second sale on the table. We show you exactly where, and what to automate first.
